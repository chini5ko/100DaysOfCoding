I was born on planet Earth, Margarita Island, Venezuela. On the island, I spoke to my parents in Cantonese, which language has more than 3000 years of history. In history class, I realized that in North America, those people speak English. Then, I moved to live the language of English in New York City wherecollege adventure started!

I chose these two majors because as a kid I saw the intersection between technology and business when my parents implemented a computer register for the store items; thus, the store productivity increases thanks to technology.

Knowing the computer history, which is very short, I am open to exploring more in multiple fields related to technology .
